Welcome to Dadong's Page!

  

My name is Jiang Dadong(蒋大东), you may also call me PatrickDD or dondonjiang. My research fields include Computer Graphics (CG) and Computer Vision (CV). Specifically, I focus on 3D reconstruction, editing, and generation (3D AIGC), including dynamic scene editing and texture generation. How far science can go ultimately depends on human cognitive abilities, that is why various 3D models (Mesh, NeRF, GS) are created to simulate the real world. I also work on real-time rendering and transmission on mobile devices in the Virtual Reality (VR) domain. I am also a photographer, and I believe that photography is a way to help me understand this world. Chinese classical literature and Buddhism have inspired me a lot, and I highly admire the wisdom of Dream of Red Mansions (红楼梦) and the Sixth Patriarch's Altar Sutra (六祖坛经).
