Welcome to Dadong's Page!

  

I am Jiang Dadong(蒋大东) from Wuxi, Jiangsu Province, you may also call me PatrickDD . My research fields include Computer Graphics (CG) and Computer Vision (CV). Specifically, I focus on 3D reconstruction, editing, and generation (3D AIGC), including dynamic scene editing and texture generation. How far science can go ultimately depends on human's cognitive abilities, that is why various 3D models (Mesh, NeRF, GS) are created to simulate the real world. I also work on real-time rendering and transmission on mobile devices in the Virtual Reality (VR) domain.
I am also a photographer, and I believe that photography is a way to help me understand this world. Chinese classical literature and Buddhism have inspired me a lot, and I highly admire the wisdom of Dream of Red Mansions and the Sixth Patriarch's Altar Sutra.
